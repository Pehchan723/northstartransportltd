Our Story

North Star Transport Ltd was founded with a clear mission: to provide reliable, professional vehicle transportation services that businesses and individuals can depend on.

Since our inception, we've grown from a small operation to become one of the UK's most trusted vehicle transportation companies, serving dealerships, individual car owners, auction houses, and businesses across the nation.

Our journey has been guided by our commitment to excellence, safety, and customer satisfaction. Just as the North Star guides travelers, we aim to be your constant guiding light in the vehicle transportation industry.
