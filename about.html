About North Star Transport

Your Trusted Partner in Vehicle Transportation Since 2022

Our Story

North Star Transport Ltd was founded in 2022 with a simple mission: to provide reliable, professional, and customer-focused vehicle transportation services across the United Kingdom.

Starting with just one vehicle and a passionate founder, we've grown to become a trusted name in the industry, serving individuals, car dealerships, auction houses, and businesses nationwide.

Our journey has been guided by our commitment to excellence, integrity, and adaptability to meet the evolving needs of our clients.

Our Mission

To be the guiding star in the vehicle transportation industry, delivering exceptional service with reliability and care. We aim to exceed expectations by ensuring safe, timely, and professional transportation of vehicles while building lasting relationships with our clients.
